Frequently asked questions

Is this financial advice?

No. Every report is general educational information based only on the numbers you enter — it isn't individualized investment advice (which in India requires a SEBI-registered investment adviser) or individualized insurance advice (which requires an IRDAI-licensed advisor or POS agent). We never recommend a specific lender, insurer, mutual fund, or policy.

What happens to the numbers I enter?

Your loan, expense, and insurance figures are used only to generate your report. Nothing is sold to lenders, insurers, or advertisers, and we don't call you afterward.

How is the free preview different from the paid report?

The free preview is a quick, on-device verdict — is your situation healthy, caution, high risk, or critical — with no payment or AI involved. The ₹199 report adds the full ranked breakdown (what to fix first and why) written out in plain language for your specific numbers.

What if I'm not sure which mode to use?

If you can list out your loans one by one, use "I can list my loans." If you want a full picture of your monthly income, expenses, insurance, and savings together, use "My full money picture." For anything else — a tax notice, an insurance claim dispute, a loan you guaranteed for someone — use "It's something else."

What if the payment goes through but the report doesn't load?

Your payment is safe either way. If a report ever fails to generate, note your payment ID from the Razorpay confirmation and contact support — the report will be sent to you directly.

Do you store my payment details?

No. Payments are handled entirely by Razorpay; this tool never sees or stores your card, UPI, or bank details.
